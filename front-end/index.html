This project is focused on providing a useful and easy to use tool to help creative professionals or non-professionals.

At some point of time every individual with a computer/mobile device might have come to a wide variety of problems ranging from blur, low resolution and aliasing in images/videos whether or not we are from a creative field. Our goal is to increase the resolution (number of pixels) in the image while at the same time adding appropriate high frequency information. In simple terms we can call it “Upscaling” of images/videos while preserving details that provide image corrections according to naked human eyes to some extent.

How to upscale image?

User needs to just drag or choose image file to upscale and confirm the images choosen. Our AI based neural netowork will generate the possible predictions of more pixels from our database trained model.

Which images give better results?

Images with less sharp edges, contrasting colors and clear textures are genreally considered as good images. They might not show some significant changes after computation.

Is upscaled image accurate in terms of real life pixel sRGB count?

It predicts the new pixels from our model generated from our dataset of images so some of the pixels may vary with actual visible pixels in real life. This is used to increased visual quality of the image.

Are images stored in any cloud/ on-premise database?

We do not store the images with us. System is open source and all the tasks happen in your own system removing the third party.

What is the ideal image size to upload?

Images above 1080p in width or height are actually considered as good density image for naked human eye. Uploading images less than 1080p may impove better in terms of overall quality.
